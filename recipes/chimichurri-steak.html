Chimichurri Steak

Description

Indulge in the mouthwatering delight of perfectly grilled ribeye steaks adorned with a zesty chimichurri sauce. This Argentine-inspired masterpiece combines succulent, seared steaks with a vibrant herb-infused blend, creating a symphony of flavors that elevate your dining experience. Get ready for a culinary journey that celebrates the art of grilling and the boldness of chimichurri!

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Chimichurri Sauce:

  • 1 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the steaks with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  2. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Grill the steaks for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, adjusting time based on your desired doneness
  4. While the steaks are grilling, prepare the chimichurri sauce.
  5. In a bowl, combine chopped parsley, minced garlic, olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  6. Once the steaks are cooked to your liking, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes.
  7. Spoon the chimichurri sauce over the steaks or serve it on the side.
  8. This is the most important step: Enjoy!
